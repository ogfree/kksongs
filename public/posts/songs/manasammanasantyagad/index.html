TRANSLATION

  1. Understanding that grief-stricken Radha’ had renounced the jealous anger in Her heart, intelligent Tungavidya’ spoke to Her the following words.

 

  1. O beautiful one, give up this jealous anger! Calmly hear my words, which were formerly spoken to Kandarpasundari, who was also anguished at heart.

 

3-4) Friend, these names of Yours were taught by Paurnamasi to Kandarpasundari, who desired to attain Your friendship.
