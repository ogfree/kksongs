LYRICS:


(1)


Adore
thy feet my Master


And
those who are devotees


Rupa
with elder and the other


Jiva,
Advaita with societies. 


 


(2)


Krsna,
Caitanya is my Lord


Radha
Krsna and Lalita accord.


Visakha
is there in the midst.


That's
my prayer in the gist.
