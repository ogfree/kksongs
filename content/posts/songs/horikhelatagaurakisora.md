TRANSLATION


1-2)
Gaura kisora plays Holi in the arms of the rasavati nari, Gadadhara. Aho! Drops
of perspiration cover Their faces, They horripilate and tears flow from Their
eyes due to intense bhava! As Gauranga sings abut Vrndavana's mellows with
Narahari-Murari, Mukunda and Vasudava Ghosa dance in ecstasy!


 


3-4)
But Gauranga repeatedly faints falling in Gadadhara's lap! And seeing them the
priya bhaktas swoon! Then coming to rest in a nikunja mandire, Gauranga faints
again after exclaiming: `where is My murali, the Yamuna and Govardhana? And
where are Vrndavana's malati, juthi and campaka flowers?' 


 


5)

a jewel mine of divine mellows!Â”
